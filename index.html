About

I am a PhD candidate in Sustainable Energy at Hamad Bin Khalifa University, Qatar, where I work as a Graduate Research Assistant on underground hydrogen (H2) storage. My research combines reservoir simulation, discrete adjoint methods, and machine learning to characterize subsurface storage of clean fuels.

I hold an MS in Physics from Bilkent University, Turkey, and a BS in Physics from Quaid-i-Azam University, Pakistan. My current work develops physics-based simulators in Python and couples them with deep generative priors — PCA and VAE parameterizations — to solve large-scale history-matching problems on channelized reservoirs.
